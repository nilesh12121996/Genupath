How it works

From job posted to application submitted — in under 8 minutes

GenuPath handles the discovery, scoring, tailoring, and submission. You review and approve before anything is sent.

01
We find the job before anyone else

GenuPath connects to Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and 45+ other job platforms via official APIs. When a new role appears, it appears in your dashboard within seconds — not after your 15-minute cron job catches it.

Push-based · No polling
02
You see your real odds before applying

Every job card shows your match score, H1B sponsorship probability from real DOL data, the company's ghosting rate, their layoff history, and how quickly they typically respond. Information no other platform gives you upfront.

Honest · Data-backed
03
One click to apply — you stay in control

You click "Prepare Application." AI tailors your resume to the exact JD language, scores it against the ATS (target 80%+), writes a cover letter, and fills the portal form. You review every change. You click Submit. Nothing goes out without your approval.
